Chocolate Lava Cake

Fourth Recipe

 Posted on February 4, 2020  |  1 minutes  |  151 words  |  

Ingredients:

  • 100 g homemade chocolate
  • 100 g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 50 g sugar
  • 50 g flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Grease and flour four small ramekins or baking cups.
  2. Melt the chocolate: Melt the homemade chocolate and butter together using a double boiler.
  3. Mix the eggs and sugar: In a bowl, whisk the eggs, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth and frothy.
  4. Combine: Add the melted chocolate-butter mixture to the egg-sugar mixture, then fold in the flour and a pinch of salt.
  5. Bake: Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins. Bake for about 12-14 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center is still soft.
  6. Serve: Let the cakes cool for 1-2 minutes, then carefully invert them onto plates. The centers should be molten and gooey—serve with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream!
